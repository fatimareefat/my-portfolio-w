Hey! What's up?

I'm Fatima Reefat - an aspiring full-stack developer and a part time writer who enjoys brainstorming ideas every once in a while. Presently a sophomore in National Institute of Technology, Warangal pursuing B Tech in Chemical engineering.
I've been in love with writing and speaking for as long as I can remember. Over time I've tried different formats of writing including but not limited to speeches, essays, poems, stories, shayaris, scripts, etc.
But when I'm not constantly scrolling through Instagram or binging web series, I'm probably reading a book or ranting about online classes which was the sole reason to start a YouTube channel.
As a content writer, I am a part of two different clubs in college, Hindi Literary & Debate Club and TEDxNITW, where I've worked on several social media posts and events.
